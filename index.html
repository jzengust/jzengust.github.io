ABOUT

I am a researcher at SenseTime Research, AI Imaging, Hong Kong.
Prior to joining SenseTime, I got my Ph.D. degree from Dept. of Electronic & Computer Engineering, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in Jan., 2018, under the jointly supervision of Prof. Khaled B. Letaief and Prof. Gene Cheung. I received my bachelor's degree from School of Electronic Science & Engineering, Nanjing University in July, 2012.

Please contact me if you are interested in internship opportunities in SenseTime Research (base Hong Kong/Shenzhen). Topics include 3D computer vision, low-level image restoration, etc.
